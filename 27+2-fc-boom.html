27+2 FC - Boom

Place two extra town centers upon hitting Castle and maintain production from all three. In Castle, set all gather points to wood, and build farms as soon as wood becomes available. Leaving villagers on gold means food can be bought if needed, or stone for a fourth town center later on. Although this type of build should usually only used on closed maps, booming is an important skill in general for any map.
