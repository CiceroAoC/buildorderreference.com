21 pop Scouts - Skirms

If an opponnent is able to mass enough archers, a transition to ranges may be needed after opening with scouts. Making your own archers is often not an option as you will just be behind in numbers, so skirmishers are often the better choice, especially since they produce faster and therefore only require one range. Researching the armour upgrade as well will make them almost impervious to archers.
