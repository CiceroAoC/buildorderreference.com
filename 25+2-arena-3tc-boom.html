25+2 Arena 3TC Boom

Particularly suited for pocket position in Arena teamgames. Buying food is needed in early castle to keep town centers working. Could switch to 4TC boom by banking wood after ~16 farms (buying 100 stone), but this delays your Imperial time. This build is used for an earlier push which doesn't require a huge economy behind it.
