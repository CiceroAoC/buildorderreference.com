21 pop Scouts

A more competitive version of the basic scouts builds, although faster builds are also common especially when luring deer or with civs with an eco bonus. In 1v1, a transition into ranged units might needed, but going straight for Castle age after making the scouts can be viable too. Scouts into knights is standard for pocket play at higher levels - though in this case you would usually want to make more scouts, and research bloodlines if possible.
