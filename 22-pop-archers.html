22 pop Archers

A more competitive version of the archers build, primarily used in team games from flank. It is also good preparation for the men-at-arms and drush builds which are more commonly used in 1v1. This build allows for two range archer production upon hitting Feudal with early fletching, with horse collar often being skipped.
