Turks 29+2+2 Fast Imperial

Gunpowder is ideal for a fast imperial as they don't require upgrades, and Turks can produce them immediately due to their free chemistry. Turks are therefore one of the few somewhat viable civs for a Fast Imperial, though be aware that this build generally less successful at higher levels where players know exactly how to respond to it! Their faster gold mining also means that they can benefit from going a bit heavier on gold and 'abusing' the market.
