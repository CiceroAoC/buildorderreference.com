27+2 Arena Scouts + Monks

An important strategy for 1v1 arena. Scouts are made to pick off enemy monks, so the player can secure the relics for themselves. This version has you transition into a boom, but more aggression options are also viable. Making more scouts and getting upgrades are also options.
