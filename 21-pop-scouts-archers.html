21 pop Scouts - Archers

This build is mostly seen in extended scout wars, as a transition to skirmishers is often the better choice vs archer builds. Archers with fletching are a powerful addition your Feudal army, but will also delay your castle time significantly. Especially nice for civs such as Huns who like to make a switch into cav archers in Castle.
